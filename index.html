Deku (One For All 100%)

A powerful all-around fighter with high mobility and devastating final moves.

Normal Abilities:

Air Force

Medium range attack that deals moderate damage and has a knockback effect.

Full Cowling

Mobility skill that increases movement speed by 50% for 10 seconds.

Delaware Smash

Close range attack with high damage and short cooldown.

Manchester Smash

Powerful kick attack that stuns enemies for a short duration.

Ultimate Abilities:

United States of Smash

AoE attack that deals massive damage to all enemies in range.

One For All: Full Cowl 100%

Temporary buff that increases all damage and speed by 100% for 15 seconds.

Detroit Smash

Massive single-target punch that can one-shot most enemies.

Oklahoma Smash

Creates a tornado that pulls in enemies and deals continuous damage.

Todoroki

Versatile fighter with both ranged and area control abilities.

Normal Abilities:

Ice Wall

Creates an ice barrier that blocks projectiles and slows enemies.

Flame Burst

Medium range flame attack that deals damage over time.

Flash Freeze

Short-range AoE that freezes enemies in place for 2 seconds.

Heat Wave

Creates a wave of heat that melts ice and burns enemies in its path.

Ultimate Abilities:

Heaven-Piercing Ice Wall

Creates a massive ice structure that damages and traps enemies.

Flashfire Fist

Unleashes a series of high-speed flame punches that deal massive damage.

Absolute Zero

Freezes an entire area, dealing damage and slowing all enemies by 80%.

Perfect Half-Cold Half-Hot

Balance of ice and fire powers, granting immunity to status effects and boosting all abilities.

Bakugo

Aggressive fighter with explosive mobility and high damage output.

Normal Abilities:

Explosion Rush

Dash attack that deals damage to enemies in path.

AP Shot

Focused explosion with high piercing damage at medium range.

Howitzer Impact

Spinning aerial attack that creates an explosion on landing.

Stun Grenade

Creates a blinding explosion that stuns enemies and reduces their accuracy.

Ultimate Abilities:

Cluster Explosion

Multiple powerful explosions around Bakugo, dealing massive AoE damage.

Gauntlet Blast

Stores up explosive power and releases it in a devastating beam attack.

Ground Zero

Creates an enormous explosion that destroys terrain and deals extreme damage to all nearby enemies.

Explosive Meteor

Launches into the air and crashes down with an explosive impact that creates a shockwave.
